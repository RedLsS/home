Frequently asked questions

Basic information for users before downloading RedLs.

Is RedLs a web app?

No. RedLs is a desktop application. The license validation is online, but the app runs locally on your computer.

Do I need a license key?

You can use the Free edition without a license. Plus and Pro features require a license key.

Can I export to Excel?

Excel export is available in Plus and Pro editions.

Can I track expenses?

Expense tracking and net profit after expenses are available in the Pro edition.

Where is my data stored?

Product, sales and expense data are stored locally on your computer. License activation is checked online.

Can I install it on multiple devices?

Device limits depend on the license issued to you. Contact support if you need more activations.
